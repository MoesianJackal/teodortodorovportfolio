About Me

Let me tell you a little about myself and what I do...

Why am I right for you?

I am a Front-End Developer looking to gain industry experience building websites and web applications. For some of my personal projects I've created Web APIs and worked with Postman on numerous occasions, and I know my way around Git/GitHub well.

As a big proponent of open-source software I use a Linux machine for a large portion of my work and I'm familiar with the Bash Shell, though I still use Windows for access to industry standard tools such as Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator.

In my teens I started making webpages at school for a few of my classes and as I realized how much I enjoy doing this it became a hobby of mine. And it remained a hobby until I realized my talents lie in Web Development, not Marine Shipping, which was my major after high school.

As such I ardently focused all of my free time on getting up to date with the latest developments in the languages I used to be familiar with and learning new ones. To support myself while learning and between changing my university, I got a position as a receptionist on a luxury cruise ship.

I learn new skills and tools exceedingly fast and am ready to become part of a determined team, where personal growth is key and bring my infatuation to a full-time role. Feel free to have a look at my work or get in touch with me!
