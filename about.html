About Me

Let me tell you a little about myself and what I do...

As an AI & IoT Support Engineer at Microsoft, I would help customers solve complex problems related to Azure Cognitive Services, Azure IoT Hub and Azure Active Directory. I used my technical expertise and communication skills to troubleshoot issues, provide guidance, and ensure customer satisfaction.

I am also pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Business/Managerial Economics at St. Cyril and St. Methodius University of Veliko Tarnovo, where I learn how to apply economic principles and methods to business decisions and strategies. Previously, I studied Marine Science/Merchant Marine Shipping at Nikola Vaptsarov Naval Academy for a year and a half, gaining valuable skills and knowledge in leadership, navigation and engineering.
